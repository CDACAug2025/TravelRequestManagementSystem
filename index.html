How to Request Travel

Follow these easy steps to submit your travel request and get approval:

1. Login

Use your registered email and password to access your dashboard.

2. Create a New Request

Go to “New Travel Request” and fill in your travel purpose, destination, and estimated cost.

3. Add Itinerary

Include travel details such as flights, hotels, or trains with dates and costs.

4. Submit for Approval

Your manager will review your request and approve or reject it.

5. Track Status

Monitor your request status and approval history from your dashboard.

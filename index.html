Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

Can I modify a travel request after submission?

Yes, you can modify pending requests. Once approved, changes require manager approval. Canceled requests can be edited and resubmitted.

What types of expenses can I include in my request?

You can include flights, accommodation, ground transportation, meals, and other business-related expenses as per your company's travel policy.

How long does approval typically take?

Approval times vary but average 1-2 business days. Urgent requests can be expedited by contacting your manager directly.
